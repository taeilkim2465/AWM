Website: Travel, Airlines, jetblue

Summary Workflows:

## open_flights_tab
Given the JetBlue booking page, this workflow opens the Flights booking panel.
- CLICK the Flights tab/button
- Wait for the Flights panel to become active (inputs and Search flights button visible)

## enter_airports
Enter origin and destination using the type-ahead suggestions.
- CLICK the From input
- TYPE: {origin-city-or-airport}
- CLICK the matching origin suggestion from the suggestions list
- CLICK the To input
- TYPE: {destination-city-or-airport}
- CLICK the matching destination suggestion from the suggestions list

## select_date_range
Pick departure and return dates in the date picker (round-trip).
- OPEN the date picker (click the depart/return date input)
- CLICK the depart date: {depart-date}
- CLICK the return date: {return-date}
- CLICK Done (or confirm) in the calendar

## set_passenger_count
Adjust number of adult passengers.
- OPEN the passenger selector (click passenger control)
- CLICK the add-adult button until the passenger count is {number-of-adults}
- CLOSE the passenger selector (if required) or ensure the count is applied

## apply_sort_and_filters
Open and apply search filters / sort options.
- CLICK Sort & Filter
- SELECT desired filter(s) or sort option(s) (e.g., origin airport checkboxes, carrier, stops)
- CLICK Apply

## choose_fare_option
Select a specific fare/class from search results.
- REVIEW fares listed for a flight result
- CLICK the fare/class button for the desired option (e.g., Economy, Business / Business Lie-Flat)

## search_and_proceed_to_checkout
Run the search and move to checkout from the selected flight/fare.
- CLICK Search flights
- From search results, CLICK the selected flight/fare entry
- CLICK Next: Checkout (or equivalent proceed-to-checkout button)
