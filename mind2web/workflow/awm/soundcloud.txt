Summary Workflows (SoundCloud)

1) perform_search
Purpose: Run a search from the site header.
Steps:
- TYPE [search-input] -> TYPE: {search-query}
- CLICK [search-submit-button]

2) open_genre_or_category
Purpose: From search results or the genre list, open a genre/category.
Steps:
- CLICK [genre-or-category-link] (e.g., Electronic, Indian classical)
- CLICK [specific-genre-result] (if a second-level genre entry appears)

3) apply_date_and_length_filters
Purpose: Narrow results by when items were added and by track length.
Steps:
- CLICK [time-filter-control] (e.g., "Added in the past month", "Past month")
- CLICK [length-filter-control] (e.g., "Any length" -> "> 30 min")

4) add_track_to_playlist_with_create_if_needed
Purpose: Add a track to an existing playlist or create the playlist if it doesn't exist.
Steps:
- CLICK [add-to-playlist-button] (on the track)
- IF playlist exists: CLICK [playlist-name] to add
  ELSE: CLICK [create-playlist-button] -> TYPE [playlist-name-input] -> TYPE: {playlist-name} -> CLICK [create/confirm]

5) play_and_like_track
Purpose: Play a track and mark it as liked.
Steps:
- CLICK [play-button] (on track or playlist entry)
- CLICK [like-button] (on the playing track or track item)

6) open_likes_list_and_view_all
Purpose: Open your Likes and view the full list.
Steps:
- CLICK [Likes-link]
- CLICK [View all likes] (or equivalent "View all" control)
