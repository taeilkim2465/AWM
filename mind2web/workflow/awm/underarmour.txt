## open_brand_or_collection

[link]  {collection-or-shop-now} -> CLICK

## refine_listing_filters

[link or button]  {filter-section: Gender} -> CLICK

[link or button]  {filter-section: Product Type} -> CLICK
[link]  {filter-option: Athletic Shoes} -> CLICK
[link or button]  {filter-section: Size} -> CLICK
[link]  {filter-option: 5.5} -> CLICK

## sort_by_price_low_to_high
Sort search results to show cheapest items first.
[button]  {sort-dropdown} -> CLICK
[option]  Price (Low - High) -> CLICK

## select_product_options_and_add_to_bag
Choose product variants (color, size) and add the item to the bag.
[link or button]  {color-option} -> CLICK
[input or radio]  {size-option} -> CLICK or TYPE
[button]  Add to Bag -> CLICK

## handle_post_add_dialog
Handle the add-to-bag confirmation dialog that appears after adding an item.
[div/dialog]  {add-to-bag-dialog appears} -> (IMPLICIT WAIT / CHECK)
[button or link]  Continue Shopping -> CLICK
OR

## apply_coupon_code
Apply a coupon code during checkout or on the cart page.
[input]  {coupon-code-field} -> TYPE: {coupon-code}
[button]  Apply -> CLICK

## add_gift_card_to_bag
