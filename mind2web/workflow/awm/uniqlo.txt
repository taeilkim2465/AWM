Summary Workflows (Uniqlo — Men shopping)

1) open_men_department
Given you are on the Uniqlo homepage, open the Men department.
- CLICK the "MEN" navigation/tab
- WAIT for the Men tab content to load (tab panel visible)

2) select_category
Select a product category (e.g., Blazers, Bottoms, Tops) within the current department.
- CLICK the "Category" filter/header
- CLICK the category option -> CLICK {category} (e.g., "Blazers", "Bottoms")

3) select_color
Filter results by color.
- CLICK the "Color" filter/header
- CLICK the color option -> CLICK {color} (e.g., "BLACK")

4) select_size
Filter results by size (handles size groups and specific size options).
- CLICK the "Size" filter/header
- CLICK the size option -> CLICK {size} (e.g., "M", "XXL", "3XL")

5) accept_modal_or_cookie
Handle interstitials, cookies, or iframe consent that block interactions.
- WAIT for consent/modal/iframe to appear
- CLICK the consent button -> CLICK "AGREE AND CONTINUE" (or equivalent accept)

6) add_top_product_to_cart
Open the first/top product in the results and add it to the cart (after selecting size if required).
- CLICK the top product in the product list (first result)
- If product page requires size selection: CLICK the desired size -> CLICK {size}
- CLICK the "Add to cart" button

Notes:
- Use these workflows as reusable building blocks in sequence (e.g., open_men_department → select_category → select_color → select_size → add_top_product_to_cart). 
- Include accept_modal_or_cookie when an iframe/consent appears before interacting with filters or product pages.
