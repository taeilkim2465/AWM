## open_submission_guidelines_overview
Description: Navigate to the Discogs submission guidelines overview page.
Steps:
1. Click the Submissions item in the marketplace/side navigation.
2. Click Submission Guidelines.
3. Click Overview Of Submission Guidelines For Releases.

## header_search_query
Description: Use the header search box to find artists, releases, or marketplace items.
Steps:
1. Focus/click the header search input.
2. Type: {search-query}.
3. Click the search/submit button (or choose a suggestion/result).

## join_community_group
Description: Find and join a community group.
Steps:
1. Click Community (or Community section in the main nav).
2. Click Groups.
3. Click Join Group (or click the group entry you want to join, then click Join Group).

## add_release_to_wantlist
Description: Add a release/album to your wantlist from its release or artist page.
Steps:
1. Open the release or the artist/release page (from search or browse).
2. Click Add to Wantlist.

## filter_marketplace_listings
Description: Filter marketplace listings by genre, format and country.
Steps:
1. Open Explore or Marketplace.
2. Click the desired genre/category (e.g., Rock).
3. Select format filter (e.g., Vinyl).
4. Select country filter (e.g., Germany).

## sort_results_most_wanted
Description: Sort a result list by Most Wanted.
Steps:
1. Open the results page (search/browse/genre view).
2. Open the Sort dropdown.
3. Select the "Most Wanted" (want,desc) sort option.
