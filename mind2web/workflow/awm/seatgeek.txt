Summary Workflows:

1) change_location_to_city
Description: Set the browsing/search location to a specific city.
Steps:
- Click the "Change Location" button.
- Type {city} into the location search input.
- Click the matching city suggestion from the results (e.g., "New York City, NY").

2) filter_events_by_month
Description: Open the date filter and select a target month for event results.
Steps:
- Click the "Filter by Date" button.
- Navigate to the desired month (click next/previous month until {target-month} is shown).
- Click "Apply" (or equivalent) to confirm the month filter.

3) open_help_and_select_topic
Description: Open the help/support area and choose a specific help article.
Steps:
- Click the "Help & Support" (or "Support") link.
- Click the desired help topic (e.g., "Buying Tickets").
