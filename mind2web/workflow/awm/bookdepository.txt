Summary Workflows (bookdepository)

1) set_search_language
Given you are on a category/search page, restrict results to a specific language.
- CLICK the language/filter link (e.g., "English") to open the language controls.
- SELECT the desired language from the language dropdown (e.g., "English").
- CLICK the "Refine results" / "Refine" button to apply the language filter.

2) open_series_or_category
Navigate into a specific series or category from the results or site navigation.
- CLICK the series/category link (e.g., "Game of Thrones") shown on the page or in the “Books by Language / Series” area.
- Wait for the series/category results page to load (page of items for that series).

3) add_top_results_to_basket_and_checkout
From a results or series page, add the top N items to the basket and proceed to checkout.
- For each of the top N results:
  - CLICK the result item to open its product page (or CLICK an "Add to basket" control if available directly on results).
  - CLICK "Add to basket" (or equivalent).
- After adding items, CLICK "Basket / Checkout" (or open the basket) to proceed to checkout.

4) continue_shopping_from_basket
When viewing the basket, return to browsing before finalizing purchase.
- CLICK "Continue Shopping" from the basket/mini-basket view to return to browsing/search pages.
- Resume browsing or repeat other workflows (e.g., open_series_or_category).
