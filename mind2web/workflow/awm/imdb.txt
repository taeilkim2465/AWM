## search_and_open_title
Given that you are on the IMDb site, this workflow finds a specific title via the search box and opens the matching result.
[input / combobox search field] -> CLICK or focus
[input / combobox search field] -> TYPE: {movie-title}
[listbox result item] -> CLICK (select the matching title)

## open_browse_menu_and_select_option
Given that you are on the IMDb site, this workflow opens the main navigation menu and selects a browse option (e.g., "Top 250 Movies").
[label or button] Menu -> CLICK
[menu item / link] {menu-item} -> CLICK

## rate_movie
Given that you are on a movie's page on IMDb, this workflow opens the rating control and submits a star rating.
[button or control] Rate -> CLICK
[button] rate {rating-value} (e.g., 10) -> CLICK
[button] Confirm / Rate -> CLICK
