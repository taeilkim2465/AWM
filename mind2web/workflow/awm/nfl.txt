## open_standings_page
Given that you are on the NFL site, this workflow opens the league standings page.
[link]  Standings -> CLICK  
[main]  Standings content -> WAIT FOR PAGE OR TAB TO LOAD

## filter_by_conference_and_division
Given that the Standings page is open, this workflow filters standings to a specific conference and division.
[button]  Conference filter -> CLICK  
[option]  {conference} (e.g., AFC) -> CLICK  
[button]  Division filter -> CLICK  
[option]  {division} (e.g., East) -> CLICK

## read_top_team_from_standings
Given that the standings are filtered to the desired division, this workflow reads the team in first place.
[section]  Standings table -> FOCUS  
[row]  First row (top team) -> READ team name and position
