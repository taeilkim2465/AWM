## sort_results_by_price_and_book
Given that you are on SpotHero search results, this workflow sorts results by price and books the top (cheapest) option.
[select]  Sort dropdown -> SELECT: Price
[ul or results list > first Book Now link]  First "Book Now" -> CLICK
