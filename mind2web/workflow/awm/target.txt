Summary Workflows:

## search_and_select_suggestion
Given a product/site search box, enter a search term and pick a suggested query/result from the dropdown.
[textbox] Search input -> TYPE: {search-term}
[link]  {matching-suggestion} -> CLICK

## open_search_result_item
From a results or category/listing page, open a specific result (product, category, job, etc.).
[link]  {result-item} -> CLICK
(optional) [link]  Explore All / See more -> CLICK

## open_filters_and_select_option
Open the filters panel and choose a specific filter category and option (color, size, type, region, etc.).
[button]  Filter / Filters -> CLICK
[button]  {filter-category-button e.g., Color / Size / Type / State/Region} -> CLICK
[checkbox|link|button]  {filter-option e.g., Black / Mega / 24 / Human Resources} -> CLICK

## sort_results_by_price_low_to_high
Change sorting to show lowest-price-first.
[button]  Sort / Price -> CLICK
[option|label]  Price - low to high -> CLICK

## add_item_to_cart
Add a found product to the cart (optionally selecting pick-up/delivery).
[button]  Add to cart -> CLICK
(optional) [tab|link|button]  Pickup & Delivery -> CLICK
(optional) [option|button]  Pick up / Fulfillment option -> CLICK
