Summary Workflows (non-overlapping, commonly reused):

## enter_flight_locations
Purpose: Enter the departure and arrival airport/city.
Steps:
- [link] From Departure Airport or City Your Origin -> CLICK
- [textbox] Origin City or Airport -> TYPE: {origin-city}
- [link] {matching-origin-popup-option} -> CLICK
- [link] To Destination Airport or City Your Destination -> CLICK
- [textbox] Destination City or Airport -> TYPE: {destination-city}
- [link] {matching-destination-popup-option} -> CLICK

## select_oneway_trip
Purpose: Switch the trip type to one-way.
Steps:
- [combobox] Trip Type:, changes will reload the page -> CLICK
- [option] One Way -> CLICK

## select_date_for_travel
Purpose: Choose a travel date from the calendar widget.
Steps:
- [button] Depart and Return Calendar (open calendar) -> CLICK
- [link] {travel-date} -> CLICK
- [button] done -> CLICK

## find_trip_by_confirmation
Purpose: Locate an existing booking using confirmation number and passenger name.
Steps:
- [tab] MY TRIPS -> CLICK
- [combobox] Find Your Trip By -> CLICK
- [option] Confirmation Number -> CLICK
- [input] Confirmation Number field -> TYPE: {confirmation-number}
- [input] First Name field -> TYPE: {first-name}
- [input] Last Name field -> TYPE: {last-name}
- [button] SUBMIT -> CLICK
