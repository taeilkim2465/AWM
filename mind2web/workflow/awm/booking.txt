## enter_destination
Given that you are on the hotel booking page, this workflow enters the destination and selects a suggested match.
- [input] Destination -> TYPE: {city, country}
- [link] {suggested-destination} -> CLICK

## select_dates_for_stay
Select check-in and check-out dates from the calendar.
- [button]  Open date picker -> CLICK
- [link]  {check-in-date} -> CLICK
- [link]  {check-out-date} -> CLICK
- [button]  Done/Close calendar -> CLICK

## set_guests_and_room
Open the occupancy selector and set the number of adults/children/rooms.
- [button]  Guests / Rooms -> CLICK
- [option/input]  Adults -> SET/TYPE: {number-of-adults}
- [option/input]  Children/Rooms (if needed) -> SET/TYPE: {numbers}
- [button]  Done/Close occupancy -> CLICK

## set_travel_purpose_work
Mark the booking as for work/business travel.
- [label/checkbox]  I'm travelling for work -> CLICK
