## Summary Workflows

## open_league_and_view_standings
Purpose: Navigate from the homepage to a league/sport page and open its standings.
- CLICK: league/sport tile (e.g., MLS, NFL, FORMULA 1, WWE)
- CLICK: STANDINGS (or the "Standings" link/tab on the league page)

## search_and_select_item
Purpose: Find a league, team, player, show, or article via the site search and open its result.
- CLICK: search input (or focus search field)
- TYPE: {query}
- CLICK: matching result card/link from the search results

## follow_entity_from_listing
Purpose: Add a league/team/player/show to your followed items from a listing or entity page.
- CLICK: Follow / Add now button on the listing or entity page
- CLICK: specific entity card or the entity's FOLLOW button (to confirm/complete follow)

## open_stories_and_share_to_facebook
Purpose: Open the site stories/articles area and share a story to Facebook.
- CLICK: Stories (or navigate to the article list / open the article)
- CLICK: Share to Facebook (share button / "share on facebook" control)
