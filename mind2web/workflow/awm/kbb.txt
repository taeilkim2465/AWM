## Workflows for kbb (Shopping, Auto)

1) open_used_cars_section  
- CLICK: "Used Car Prices" (or "Price New/Used") link/menu item  
- WAIT/VERIFY: wait for the used-cars listing or the filters pane (e.g., "Sort By" dropdown) to appear

2) select_car_make  
- CLICK: "Filter by Make" or the makes list/menu  
- CLICK: the specific make name -> CLICK {Make} (e.g., Audi, Mercedes-Benz)

3) filter_to_used_condition  
- OPEN: condition filters panel (if collapsed) or reveal filters in the sidebar  
- CLICK: "Used" checkbox/label to enable filtering to used vehicles

4) set_year_range  
- OPEN: year or model-year filter control (e.g., year-range selector)  
- SELECT/CLICK: the desired range or endpoints (e.g., "Before 2015" or "2004 - 2012") and APPLY/confirm

5) sort_by_price  
- CLICK: "Sort By" dropdown  
- SELECT: "Price: Low to High" or "Price: High to Low" (parameterize as {order})

Each workflow is a reusable subroutine that can be composed to perform the full tasks (e.g., open used cars -> select make -> set year range -> filter to used -> sort by price).
