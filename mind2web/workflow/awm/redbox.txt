Summary Workflows for Redbox (Entertainment, Movie, redbox)

## open_on_demand_section
Given you are on the Redbox site, open the On Demand area.
- [link] On Demand (nav) -> CLICK
- [label] On Demand (page/section) -> CLICK (or verify loaded)

## open_supported_devices_and_select_streaming
Find supported devices and view streaming-device category (including Free Live TV).
- [link] Supported Devices -> CLICK
- [link] Streaming Devices (or similar) -> CLICK
- [link] Free Live TV -> CLICK

## search_for_title_from_header
Search for a specific movie/title using the header search and pick the suggestion.
- [input] Header search box -> TYPE: {movie-title}
- [li] Suggested result for {movie-title} -> CLICK

## rent_selected_on_demand_title
Rent the currently selected On Demand title (choose format if offered).
- [button] Rent On Demand -> CLICK
- [option/button] Select format (e.g., High Definition) -> CLICK
- [button] Confirm / Rent / Continue -> CLICK

## browse_new_releases_and_filter_by_genre
Browse newly released or Movies To Rent and filter by a genre (e.g., Comedy).
- [link] Movies To Rent / New (On Demand navigation) -> CLICK
- [link/button] Genres -> CLICK
- [button] Filter by {genre} (e.g., Comedy) -> CLICK

Each workflow is a reusable subroutine; combine them in sequence as needed (e.g., search_for_title_from_header -> rent_selected_on_demand_title).
