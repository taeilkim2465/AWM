## choose_sail_to_region
Given that you are on the cruise search page, this workflow sets the cruise destination region.
[link]  Sail To -> CLICK
[button]  {region-name} -> CLICK

## choose_sail_from_port
Given that you are on the cruise search page, this workflow sets the departure port or city.
[link]  Sail From -> CLICK
[button]  {departure-port-or-city} -> CLICK

## select_dates_and_duration
Given that you are on the cruise search page, this workflow chooses the month (or date range) and the cruise length.
[link]  Dates -> CLICK
[button]  {month-year} -> CLICK
[button]  {duration-option (e.g., 10+ Days)} -> CLICK

## submit_cruise_search
Given that you have configured destination, departure, dates and duration, this workflow runs the search.
[button]  SEARCH CRUISES -> CLICK
