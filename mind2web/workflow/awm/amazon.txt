Summary Workflows:

## open_gift_cards_category
Given you are on Amazon's site, this workflow opens the Gift Cards category from the main navigation.
- Click the main navigation/menu (e.g., "All" or categories) -> CLICK
- Click "Gift Cards" -> CLICK

## filter_gift_cards_by_ocasion_and_price
Apply common filters to narrow gift card search results.
- Click the occasion filter (e.g., "Congratulations") -> CLICK
- Click the price range filter (e.g., "$50 to $100") -> CLICK

## choose_denomination_and_quantity
Select a specific gift card value and set how many to buy.
- Select the denomination option/button (e.g., "$50") -> CLICK
- Set the Quantity field to {quantity} -> TYPE: {quantity}

## add_item_to_cart
Add the selected gift card(s) to the shopping cart.
- Click "Add to cart" -> CLICK
- (Optional follow-up) Click "Cart" or proceed to checkout as needed -> CLICK/VIEW

Notes:
- Use {denomination} and {quantity} as placeholders to vary the workflow (e.g., $50 and 2).
