Summary Workflows (KOA website)

1) open_deals_hot_deals
Description: Navigate to the Hot Deals section under Deals.
- [link] Deals -> CLICK
- [link] Hot Deals -> CLICK

2) enter_location_and_select_suggestion
Description: Fill the "Where do you want to go?" field and choose an autocomplete result.
- [textbox] Where do you want to go? -> TYPE: {city_or_zip}
- [link] {autocomplete_suggestion} -> CLICK

3) set_show_results_within_radius
Description: Choose the search radius for Hot Deals / results.
- [label/select] Show results within -> CLICK
- [option] {radius_option} (e.g., 250 Miles) -> CLICK

4) pick_checkin_and_checkout_dates
Description: Select a check-in date and set a check-out date for a stay.
- [button or input] Check In / Depart calendar -> CLICK
- [link] {check-in-date} -> CLICK
- [textbox or input] Check Out -> TYPE or CLICK: {check-out-date} (or select the check-out date in the calendar)

5) open_filters_and_search
Description: Open advanced filters (if needed) and submit the search/find results.
- [link] Filters / Advanced Search -> CLICK
- [button] Find a KOA / Submit / Search -> CLICK

Use these workflows as reusable subroutines (plug in the specific city/zip, dates, radius, or button label as needed).
