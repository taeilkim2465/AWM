## open_inventory_search
Given you are on the CarMax site, open the vehicle inventory search.
[link or button] Shop / Search Cars -> CLICK
[input] Search make, model, or keyword -> CLICK (focus)

## enter_search_keyword_and_choose_suggestion
Type a vehicle make/model/keyword and pick the suggested item.
[input] Search make, model, or keyword -> TYPE: {make/model/keyword}
[link or menuitem] {suggested-make-or-model} -> CLICK

## choose_vehicle_type_or_category
Switch to a specific vehicle category (e.g., Trucks, Pickup Trucks).
<header or main> Trucks / Pickup Trucks -> CLICK
[link or button] Pickup Trucks (or other category) -> CLICK

## open_filter_panel
Open the filters area to refine results.
[button] Filters / Browse By / (panel) -> CLICK
[aside or region] Filter panel visible -> CLICK (focus)

## filter_by_make
Filter results by one or more vehicle makes.
[button] Make -> CLICK
[checkbox] {Make Name} -> CLICK
[checkbox] {Another Make} -> CLICK

## filter_by_year
Restrict results to a specific model year.
[button] Year -> CLICK
[menuitem or button] {year} -> CLICK

## filter_by_price_max
Set a maximum price for results.
[button] Price -> CLICK
[menuitem or button] Max price {value} -> CLICK

## filter_by_mileage
Limit results by mileage.
[button] Mileage -> CLICK
[option or menuitem] {max-mileage-option} -> CLICK

## filter_by_exterior_color
Show vehicles with a specific exterior color.
[button] Exterior Color -> CLICK
[link or menuitem] {color} -> CLICK

## set_distance_and_location
Search within a distance or around a ZIP/city.
[button] Distance & Shipping / Distance -> CLICK
[input] Location / ZIP -> TYPE: {zip or city}
[button or apply] Apply / Update results -> CLICK

## sort_results
Change the result ordering (best match / most recent).
[button] Sort by -> CLICK
[option] {Best match | Most recent | Other} -> CLICK
