## Workflow: open_profile_editor
Purpose: Open the user profile/edit form so fields (birthday, allergies, bio) are available.
Steps:
1. CLICK the profile/menu button.
2. WAIT for the profile editor form or profile fields (birthday select, allergy controls, bio textarea) to appear.

## Workflow: set_birthday
Purpose: Select day and year for the user's birthday.
Steps:
1. CLICK the birthday day select control.
2. CLICK the desired day option -> TYPE/SELECT: {day}
3. CLICK the birthday year select control.
4. CLICK the desired year option -> TYPE/SELECT: {year}

## Workflow: add_allergy_and_note
Purpose: Mark an allergy and provide an allergy note for the restaurant.
Steps:
1. CLICK the allergy checkbox for the allergen -> e.g., Peanuts.
2. CLICK the allergy notes textarea.
3. TYPE: {allergy-note} (e.g., "Dairy allergy â€” severe; please notify kitchen")

## Workflow: update_bio_and_save
Purpose: Edit the profile bio and persist changes.
Steps:
1. CLICK the bio/profile notes textarea.
2. TYPE: {bio-text} (e.g., "Love ramen noodles")
3. CLICK the Save/submit button.
4. WAIT for a save confirmation or that the profile editor closes.
